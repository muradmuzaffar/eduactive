About Us

Eduactive is a user-friendly platform that uses special algorithm to help students find the perfect university programs for them. We have a big database that includes the top 1000 universities, and we make sure to keep this information up-to-date. Our goal is to help students make smart choices about their education and future careers. We do this by matching students with university programs based on their backgrounds, interests, budgets, locations, and career goals. This way, students can explore a list of programs that are just right for them.On our platform, students can find important details about universities and their programs, like tuition fees, how long the programs are, what it takes to get in, and rankings. We also cover a wide range of subjects, so everyone can find something they're interested in.
